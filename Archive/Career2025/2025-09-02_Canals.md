## Company Overview

### Company Profile
- **Age:** 2.5 years old
- **Size:** 45 people total (~35 engineers)
- **Business:** AI for wholesale distributors
- **Client Engagement:** All clients on 3-year contracts

### Business Model & Vision
- **Target Market:** Wholesale distributors with 3+ million products
- **Problem:** Distributors use ERP systems (very hated but accepted)
- **Pain Point:** Transition cost from ERP is too great to switch
- **Product Confusion:** Same parts have different part numbers across databases/companies
- **Vision:** "Be Amazon for wholesale distributors"

### Product Focus
- **Order Processing:** Manual order placement (voice orders, email orders)
- **Parsing:** Parse incoming orders
- **Matching:** Match orders with catalog
- **End-to-End:** Beginning of workflow is taking orders from distributors
