# Partnership Strategy: BestInterest

**Date**: 2026-03-16
**Status**: Exploring

---

## Thesis

BestInterest and our product have zero feature overlap — they own communication/AI coaching, we own finance/scheduling. A partnership (integration or co-marketing) could create a full-stack OFW alternative at lower cost without either side building outside their core.

---

## What BestInterest Brings

- **AI communication**: Message Shield (inbound filter), Tone Guardian (outbound coaching), AI Coach
- **Solo Mode**: Works without co-parent cooperation via dedicated phone number routing — critical for DV survivors
- **Founder**: Sol Kennedy — YC S06 alum, ex-Head of Product at Dropcam ($555M acquisition by Google/Nest), Google PM, angel investor in Coinbase/GitLab/Instacart. Likely $5-20M+ personal wealth self-funding the company.
- **Advisor**: Dr. Ramani Durvasula — 1.5M+ YouTube subscribers, leading voice on narcissism. Major discovery driver for high-conflict audience.
- **User base**: 9,000+ users, 126 iOS ratings (4.6 stars), ~17 months old
- **Revenue**: Estimated $81-162K ARR (very early stage)
- **Press**: WIRED feature, 4 wire releases, 6+ podcast appearances
- **Court status**: Claims court-ordered but unverified at scale

## What We Bring

- **Payments**: Stripe ACH — no $10K cap (unlike OFW's Dwolla), same-day ACH possible, supports up to $500K/txn
- **AI expense tracking + splitting**: Auto-categorization, receipt scanning, shared expense ledger
- **Shared custody calendar**: With AI schedule swap detection (white space — nobody does this)
- **Court-admissible financial records**: Timestamped, unalterable transaction history
- **Domain expertise**: Personal experience with OFW's limitations ($10K/mo spousal support blocked), built full finance pipeline (SQLite, 5,620+ CC transactions, 3,218 Amazon orders, payslip ingestion)

## Why Partnership Makes Sense

1. **Zero feature overlap**: Communication vs. finance/scheduling is a clean split
2. **Different founder strengths**: Kennedy is product/consumer, we bring fintech/data engineering
3. **No payments on BestInterest roadmap**: 17 months, 15 versions, zero financial features built
4. **Monetization lever for BestInterest**: At ~$81-162K ARR, a payments feature taking a cut of transactions could dramatically increase their ARPU
5. **Shared enemy**: OFW charges $150-$300/yr with 1.4-star TrustPilot rating — both products undercut and outperform OFW in their respective domains
6. **Distribution synergy**: Dr. Ramani's 1.5M YouTube audience + mandatory parenting class partnerships (untapped channel, 17 states)
