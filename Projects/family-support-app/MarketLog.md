### Entry #1 — 2026-03-15 — Phase 1: Research
**Agent**: Researcher (Opus)
**Input**: AI-powered coparenting app to compete with OurFamilyWizard — cheaper pricing, AI-assisted schedule swaps via chat, ACH payments via Stripe
**Output**: Market-Research-Brief.md
**Key findings**:
- OFW: $10.5M revenue, ~52K active subscribers (not 1M), 1.5-star SiteJabber rating — entrenched but deeply disliked
- $440M global market growing at 12.4% CAGR; US ~$176M
- OFW's payment feature has 90%+ gross margin — ACH costs $0.80-$5/txn vs $150-$300/yr charged per parent
- Three AI-native entrants since late 2024 (BestInterest, DivorceX, Parent Copilot) — none combine AI + payments + scheduling at low price
- No existing product offers AI schedule swap detection — genuine white space
- Stripe Connect eliminates money transmission licensing burden
- Court moat is relationship-based, not certification-based — biggest unknown is how many orders name OFW specifically
