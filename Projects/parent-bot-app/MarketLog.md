## Process Log (Append-Only)

### Entry #1 — 2026-03-16 — Phase 1: Research
**Agent**: Researcher (Opus)
**Input**: Idea doc at Projects/parent-bot-app/idea.md — AI parent avatar chatbot with voice cloning, customizable personality, educational features (dyslexia, ADHD), targeting divorced/co-parenting families
**Output**: Projects/parent-bot-app/Market_Research_Brief.md
**Key findings**:
- No direct competitor combines parent voice cloning + educational modes + coparenting context
- 22M children in divided US families; $23M ARR at 1% penetration ($15/mo)
- COPPA 2025 amendments classify voiceprints as personal information — #1 regulatory risk
- Character.AI teen suicide lawsuits + Moxie $60M hardware failure = cautionary tales
- Recommended MVP: parent-voice bedtime stories first, expand to tutoring then open chat
- 10 evidence gaps identified (all UNKNOWN), most critical: would kids engage with parent-voice AI vs generic?
