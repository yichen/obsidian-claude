# Club Connection Summer Camp at Grand Ridge Elementary

- **Dates**: June 22 - August 14, 2026
- **Hours**: 7AM - 6PM
- **Cost**: $395/week
- **Grades**: Incoming 1st - 5th graders
- **Location**: Grand Ridge Elementary, 1739 NE Park Dr, Issaquah, WA 98029
- **Schools Served**: Grand Ridge, Endeavour, Cascade Ridge & Challenger
- **Registration**: Online, March 13 - 31, 2026
- **Late pickup fee**: $5/min

## What to Bring Every Day

- Sack lunch
- Water bottle
- Swim suit, towel
- Sunscreen
- ID for pickup

## Important Notes

- Enrichment (outside groups) & field trips are subject to change
- Field trips depart at 9:30 AM and return by 4:00 PM unless otherwise noted
- Campers are required to attend field trips listed with their 26-27 school year grade
