# !MPACT Summer Camp at Pine Lake Middle School

- **Dates**: June 22 - August 14, 2026
- **Hours**: 7AM - 6PM
- **Cost**: $395/week (*Week 2 is $320)
- **Grades**: Incoming ISD 6th - 8th graders
- **Location**: Pine Lake Middle School, 3095 Issaquah-Pine Lake Rd SE, Sammamish, WA 98075
- **Registration**: March 13 - 31, 2026
- **Website**: isd411.org/programs-services/basc/breaks-and-summer
- **Late pickup fee**: $5/min

## Why !MPACT?

Designed with middle schoolers in mind, our program blends structured experiences with choice-based activities, creating a summer they'll never forget. Each week, campers enjoy three exciting field trips plus a variety of specialized activities led by our dedicated team.

## What to Bring Every Day

- Sack lunch
- Water bottle
- Swim suit, towel
- Sunscreen
- ID for pickup

## Important Notes

- Field trips occur every Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday
- Field trips typically depart at 9:30 AM and return by 4:00 PM
