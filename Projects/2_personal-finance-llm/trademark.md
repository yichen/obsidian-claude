# Trademark & Domain Report: RebalanceFi

**Date**: 2026-03-17
**Product**: AI-powered personal finance app — granular spending tracking with line-item categorization (Amazon orders, Costco receipts, etc.)
**Comparable products**: Mint, Simplifi by Quicken, Monarch Money, YNAB
**Core differentiator**: Item-level transaction categorization (e.g., tagging individual Amazon line items by category instead of "$347.52 - Amazon")
**Target audience**: People under cashflow stress who need to understand exactly where their money goes
**Prior report**: See [Trademark-Review.md](Trademark-Review.md) for earlier naming candidates (trove, fathom, grain)

---

## Chosen Name

- **App name**: Rebalance
- **Domain**: rebalancefi.com
- **Domain status**: AVAILABLE (verified 2026-03-17 via WHOIS)
- **Name rationale**: "Rebalance" captures the emotional journey of the target user — recovering financial balance after being cashflow-negative. The "fi" suffix signals finance and financial independence (FI/FIRE community). The app unifies spending tracking and investment tracking, which maps to "rebalancing your entire financial life."

### Rebalance360 (Investment Management Firm)

- **Website**: rebalance360.com
- **Business**: Low-cost investment advisory / portfolio management
- **Domain**: rebalance.com (registered since 1999, currently not resolving — ECONNREFUSED)
- **USPTO trademark**: **Not found** — no registration for "Rebalance" or "Rebalance360" in Class 36
- **Conflict assessment**: LOW risk. They operate in investment advisory (managing portfolios), not spending tracking/budgeting. Different product category. No registered trademark to enforce. The name "Rebalance" is descriptive/generic in investing contexts, making it harder to claim exclusive rights.

## Branding Assessment

### Strengths

- **Emotional resonance**: "Rebalance" = recovering from financial stress, getting back on track
- **Dual meaning**: Portfolio rebalancing (investing) + life rebalancing (spending/budgets) — perfect for a unified app
- **Community signal**: "Rebalance" is core Bogleheads vocabulary, signals financial literacy
- **Speakability**: Easy to say, no spelling ambiguity, passes the podcast/radio test
- **"fi" suffix**: Works double duty — finance + financial independence (FIRE community)

### Risks

- First impression may skew "investment tool" — marketing/landing page must immediately show spending tracking + budgets + investments
- Rebalance360 exists as an investment advisory firm (but no trademark, different product)

### Comparison to Competitors

| App | Name Strategy |
|-----|--------------|
| Mint | Metaphor (fresh, money/mint) |
| YNAB | Value prop acronym ("You Need A Budget") |
| Monarch | Metaphor (control, oversight) |
