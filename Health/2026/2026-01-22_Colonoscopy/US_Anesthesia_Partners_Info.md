# US Anesthesia Partners of WA (USAP)

**Swedish Health Services** is excited to have US Anesthesia Partners of WA (USAP) providing high-quality anesthesia services to our patients.

USAP provides both excellent anesthesia care and customer service to our patients. USAP will be working directly with your insurance company for payment related to the anesthesia portion of your services. The reimbursable amount and the portion that you will be responsible for paying directly to USAP will be dependent on your benefit levels, your plan coverage guidelines and your insurance plan maximum and deductibles.

If you have additional questions once your claim is processed and you receive your EOB (Explanation of Benefits) please feel free to contact USAP directly at the number below.
