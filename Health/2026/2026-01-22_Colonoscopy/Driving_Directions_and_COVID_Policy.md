## Driving Instructions

**Traveling East on I-90**
1.  Take the E. Sunset Way-Highlands Drive exit (No. 18).
2.  Go left at the “Y” and continue onto Highlands Drive.
3.  Turn left (west) at the first traffic light onto NE Discovery Drive.
4.  Turn left (south) at the next light onto 8th Avenue NE.
5.  Travel down 8th Avenue, until it turns into the Swedish Issaquah main entrance.
6.  Immediately turn right to enter the underground parking garage. Parking is also available on the surface lots.

# COVID-19 Escort Policy – Issaquah

**Swedish Gastroenterology**

*   **You may check in with ONE (1) support person/escort.** Support person/escort must be 18 years old or older.
    *   The hospital can accommodate caregivers for patients who need extra help physically or cognitively.
    *   If you do not speak English and would like your escort to accompany you to help interpret, the hospital can accommodate this as well.
    *   **The hospital has video interpreters available if needed.**
    *   Escorts must stay nearby the hospital.

*   If patients do not have an escort, they can use a certified Cabulance service. Please inquire information from the Swedish Gastroenterology Clinic staff.
*   Please provide either the certified Cabulance or escort’s phone number to the endoscopy center when checking in so they can call them once the procedure is done.

*   Once done with your procedure, you will receive your instructions and report in writing.
    *   Please have your escort stay nearby the hospital while waiting.
    *   **Parking is FREE at the Issaquah campus.**
    *   The escort will be called and asked to pick you up and provide instructions on where.
    *   If escorts are unable to come into the building, arrangements can be made for endoscopy staff to meet escorts at a designated location.
    *   If your escort has any additional questions or needs any information, the recovery RN will gladly contact them by phone to discuss.
