## 📅 Preparation Timeline

### 7 Days Prior (Jan 15)
*   **Escort:** Arrange for a responsible adult (18+) to drive you home. **Mandatory.**
*   **Diet:** Begin **LOW FIBER DIET**.

    | Category | **✅ Okay to Eat** | **❌ Do Not Eat** |
    | :--- | :--- | :--- |
    | **Milk & Dairy** | Milk, Cream, Hot Chocolate, Buttermilk, Cheese, Sour Cream | Yogurt mixed with seeds/nuts/granola/fruit skins. |
    | **Breads & Grains** | Breads/grains made with refined white flour. White rice, Plain crackers, Low-fiber cereal (cream of wheat/corn flakes). | Brown/wild rice. Whole grain bread/rolls/pasta/crackers. Whole-grain/high-fiber cereal (granola, oatmeal). Bread with nuts/seeds. |
    | **Meat & Seafood** | Chicken, Turkey, Lamb, Lean Pork, Veal, Fish/Seafood (except salmon). | **Salmon**. Tough meat with gristle. |
    | **Legumes** | *(None listed)* | Dried peas, Dried beans, Lentils, Any other legumes. |
    | **Fruits** | Juice w/o pulp. Apple sauce. Canned/cooked fruit w/o seeds/skins. Cantaloupe/Honeydew. Peeled apricots/peaches. | Fruits with seeds/skins/membranes (berries, pineapple, apples, oranges, watermelon). Dried fruit. Canned/cooked fruit with seeds. |
    | **Nuts & Seeds** | Creamy (smooth) nut butters. | Nuts. Chunky nut butter. Seeds (fennel, sesame, pumpkin, sunflower). |
    | **Fats & Oils** | Butter, Margarine, Vegetable/other oils, Mayonnaise, Salad dressing w/o seeds/nuts. | Salad dressing with seeds or nuts. |
    | **Desserts** | Custard, Plain pudding, Ice Cream (w/o nuts/fruit), Sorbet/Sherbet, Jell-O (**not red/blue**), Cookies/cake with white flour (w/o seeds/nuts/fruit). | Coconut. Anything with seeds/nuts. Anything with **red or blue dye**. Made with whole grain flour. |
    | **Soups** | Broth, bouillon, consommé, strained soups. Milk/cream-based soup (strained). | Unstrained soups. Soups with herbs. Chili. Lentil/Pea/Dried bean/Corn soup. |
    | **Vegetables** | Canned/cooked w/o seeds/skins. Potatoes w/o skins. | Raw vegetables. Corn. Potatoes with skin. Cucumbers. Tomatoes. Cooked cabbage/Brussels sprouts. Green peas. Squash. Lima beans. Onion. |
    *   **AVOID:** Nuts, seeds, popcorn, and fiber supplements (Metamucil, Citrucel).
*   **Medications:**
    *   **Blood Thinners:** Follow specific instructions if applicable. Call 206-215-4250 if unsure.
    *   **Diabetes/Weight Loss:** See [Medication Management](#medication-management) section below.
    *   **Hold:** Herbal supplements, oily capsules, Pepto-Bismol, Iron supplements, Chinese remedies.
    *   **Hold:** Oral Naltrexone (7 days prior).

### 3 Days Prior (Jan 19)
*   **Diet:** **Avoid red, blue, or purple dyes** in foods/drinks.

### 1 Day Prior (Jan 21) - PREP DAY
> [!WARNING]
> **NO Alcohol, Marijuana, or Illicit Drugs** (24 hours prior).

*   **Diet:** **STOP solid foods.** Begin **CLEAR LIQUID DIET** all day.

    | **You may have ONLY CLEAR LIQUIDS, such as:** | **DO NOT DRINK:** |
    | :--- | :--- |
    | ✓ Black coffee or tea (sugar is OK) | ✗ **Milkshakes, Smoothies** |
    | ✓ Soda (clear or cola is OK) | ✗ **Milk** (dairy or non-dairy) |
    | ✓ Clear sports drinks: Gatorade; Pedialyte | ✗ **Juice that is not clear** (orange, pineapple, grapefruit, tomato) |
    | ✓ Popsicles or Jell-O (**not red or blue**) | ✗ **Cooked cereal** |
    | ✓ Fruit juice (apple, white grape, white cranberry) | ✗ **Barium/Oral contrast** |
    | ✓ Bouillon/broth | ✗ **Alcohol** |
    | ✓ Water (flavored water/coconut water) | |
*   **Prep Solution (SUFLAVE):**
    1.  Open 1 flavor packet and pour into provided bottle.
    2.  Fill with lukewarm water to line. Shake well.
    3.  Refrigerate for best taste.
    4.  Repeat for 2nd bottle.

*   **At 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM (First Dose):**
    *   Drink first bottle (8 oz every 15 mins until empty).
    *   Drink at least **16 oz of water** following the prep.
    *   Continue clear liquids until bedtime.

### Day of Procedure (Jan 22)
*   **6 Hours Prior to Check-in (2:00 AM):**
    *   Drink **second bottle** of SUFLAVE (8 oz every 15 mins until empty).
    *   Drink **16 oz of water** following the prep.
    *   Finish water by **3 hours** before check-in.
    *   *Goal: Stool should be clear yellow, like urine.*

*   **3 Hours Prior to Check-in (5:00 AM):**
    *   **STOP EVERYTHING BY MOUTH.** No water, gum, mints, or medications.

## 💊 Medication Management

### Diabetes Medications

| Medication Type | 1 Day Before | Day of Procedure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Long-Acting Insulin** (Lantus, Levemir, etc.) | Take **50%** of usual dose | Take **50%** of usual dose |
| **Rapid/Short-Acting Insulin** (Humalog, Novolog) | Usual dose AM. **DO NOT take** once on clear liquids. | **DO NOT TAKE** |
| **Mixed Insulin** (70/30, 75/25) | Usual dose AM. **Take 50%** of PM dose. | **DO NOT TAKE** |
| **Metformin** | Usual dose AM. **DO NOT take** once on clear liquids. | **DO NOT TAKE** |
| **Oral Meds** (Glipizide, Januvia, etc.) | Usual dose AM. **DO NOT take** once on clear liquids. | **DO NOT TAKE** |

### Weight Loss / GLP-1 Agonists
*   **Weekly Injection** (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Trulicity): **HOLD for 7 days prior.**
*   **Daily Injection** (Saxenda, Victoza): **HOLD for 24 hours prior.**
*   **Oral** (Phentermine, Qsymia): **HOLD for 4 days prior.**

### Other Medications
| **Medication Type** | **Instructions** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Herbal supplements, oily capsules, Pepto-Bismol, Iron supplements, Chinese remedies, excess vitamins & elements*** | **HOLD for 7 days** before procedure.<br>*(Multivitamins are OK)* |
| **Naltrexone** | **Hold ORAL Naltrexone** 7 days before.<br>**Hold INJECTABLE Naltrexone** 4 weeks before.<br>*(If prescribed for opioid addiction, OK to continue)* |
| **NSAIDS** (Aspirin, ibuprofen, Celebrex, etc.) | Can **safely be continued** in the pre-procedure period.<br>*Caution is recommended in the early post-procedure period due to increased risk of bleeding. Your endoscopist will give further instructions.* |
| **Diuretics** (Furosemide, Spironolactone) | **HOLD on day of procedure.** |
| **Blood Pressure Meds** | Generally okay, but check with provider if unsure. |

## 🚨 Hypoglycemia (Low Blood Sugar) Protocol
*   **Symptoms:** Shakiness, sweating, confusion, dizziness, rapid heartbeat.
*   **Action:**
    1.  Check blood sugar.
    2.  If low (<70): Drink 4 oz apple juice/regular soda OR eat small Jell-O (NOT red/purple).
    3.  Wait 15 mins and recheck.
    4.  Repeat if needed.

## 🚗 After Procedure
*   **Transportation:** Responsible adult (18+) **MUST** be present to drive you. Uber/Lyft/Taxi is **NOT allowed** unless accompanied by your escort.
*   **Activity:** No driving for 12 hours. No alcohol/sedatives for 24 hours.
*   **Travel:** Avoid air travel/remote areas for 2 weeks if large polyps removed (bleeding risk).
