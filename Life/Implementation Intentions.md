# Implementation Intentions

"Implementation Intentions" (If-Then planning) bridge the gap between knowing what to do and actually doing it when emotions run high.

## Work & Professional

### Handling Feelings of Undervalue or Disrespect
**Trigger:** When I feel undervalued or provoked by management (e.g., Ben).
**Intention:**
> "If I feel undervalued or provoked, then I will take a deep breath to honor my own feelings internally, but externally I will become a 'Grey Rock'—calm, boring, and strategically brief."

**The "Grey Rock" Strategy:**
- **If criticized:** Do not defend. Say, "I see where you're coming from. I'll note that down."
- **If they are wrong:** Do not prove them wrong immediately. Say, "That's an interesting perspective. Let me look into the data on that."
- **Goal:** Separate internal worth from external performance. validation.
