ChatGPT did say Adler would challenge me to ask:

From Adlerian angle, insomnia is not random, it reflects psychic tension between competing goals:
- I want growth and mastery (AI, dating, creativity)
- I also need stability and recovery (sleep, routine, parenting)
ChatGPT recommends that I follow Adlerian's approach as the following, which shifts me from reacting to creating, which is Adler's definition of mental health:
- *Naming my current self-chosen emotion*:"I choose excitement and restlesness because I'm trying to become someone new
