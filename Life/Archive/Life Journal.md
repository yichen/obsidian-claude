### 2023-06-09 #china
- Back from an almost 3 week vacation in China
- Shenzhen is hot in May and June. The best time to visit is in November and December.
- It is important to have a personal ID, a cell number, and a bank account. I re-opened my old Bank of China bank card, and created a new cell number 13682481975 from China Mobile. I should call the Hubei Police Station regarding applying for a personal ID.
- I am able to sleep very well after the week of jet lag in China vacation. That is, I can nap after lunch. I usually won't be able to nap when not on vacation.
- I am too lazy. Note how Zhang Yi and Chen Kai taking kids out to play for a full day without draining. I feel reluctant to drive for 30 minutes for an activity.
- Laurence got sick from traveling to China, without coughing (支气管炎), treated with 雾化 in China. He was coughing again after returning to USA.
- It's always sad to leave China. Both mom and dad will cry. After arriving USA, my mom's mood appears to be good when I call back.
- On the flight to and from China (Asiana and JPL airlines), I can now order wifi on flight on 24-hour window (cover the whole leg as long as both flights happens on the same airline)
- I tend to buy more stuff during vacation. I bought the Leica Q3 during my vacation when it was announced on May 26, and bought a Fjallraven shorts for the summer because of the hot weather in Shenzhen.
- I tend to be more interested in fitness during vacation. For example, I have been paying attention to xiaohongshu.com fitness posts.
- I got very excited about China's change for the past 4 years. For example, pay everything with a smart phone (WeChat Pay, Alipay), and how convenient it is to ship things. For example, post office will come pick up books I bought and want to be shipped.
- We used the LetsVPN which turned out very well.
