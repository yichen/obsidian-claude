## Lessons Learned

### Driving & Traffic

⚠️ **Friday Afternoon I-5 Southbound = Terrible:**
- Experienced bad traffic on this trip
- Previously saw same issue going to Great Wolf Lodge with kids
- **Solution:** Leave in the morning instead of Friday afternoon
- Avoid Friday afternoon southbound I-5 at all costs

### Connectivity

📡 **Cell Service:**
- **No cell service** in campground
- **But:** Parking areas have Starlink service available
- Plan work/communication accordingly
