## Decision Not to Stay

### Reasons for Leaving:
1. Insufficient privacy between sites
2. Side-by-side parking lot configuration
3. Too close to neighboring campers

### What Would Have Made Me Stay:
- [ ] If I had brought the **Oztent** (for privacy)
- [ ] If I had brought a **cooking pot for dinner**

---

## Lessons Learned

### Privacy Requirements
💡 **Privacy is Critical for Solo Camping:**
- Side-by-side parking lot configuration doesn't work for me
- Need visual barriers between sites
- Oztent would have provided needed privacy

### Essential Gear for Comfort
📝 **Bring for Future Visits:**
- **Oztent** - Provides privacy in crowded campgrounds
- **Cooking pot** - For dinner preparation
- Consider sites with more natural separation

### Site Selection
⚠️ **Research Campground Layout:**
- South Campground has tight spacing
- Check campground maps for site spacing before booking
- Look for sites with natural barriers (trees, bushes)
- Avoid side-by-side parking lot style configurations
