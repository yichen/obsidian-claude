## Trails I Hiked in this trip
- [Sleep Giant East Trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/hawaii/kaua-i--2/sleeping-giant-nounou-mountain-east-trail). I hiked it on my first day
- Awa'awapuhi Trail, the best hike I did. Met a young man (28yo) from Netherland and had a long talk (~4 hour) and had lunch together
- [Canyon Trail to Waipoo Falls](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/hawaii/kaua-i--2/canyon-trail-to-waipo-o-falls). It turns out I have hiked this place with Fabio in 2016
- Bee took me to hike the [Kuilau Ridge Trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/hawaii/kaua-i--2/kuilau-ridge-trail)
- [Maha'ulepu Heritage Trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/hawaii/kaua-i--2/maha-ulepu-heritage-trail-shipwreck-beach-to-punahoa-point)
- I took a stroll at the Kapaa Beach Park
