## Itinerary: Half-Day Red Rock Canyon Hike

### 1:30 PM – Arrive in Las Vegas & Pick Up Rental Car
- Grab a quick snack or water before heading out

### 2:00 PM – Drive to Red Rock Canyon Visitor Center
- Stop at Visitor Center (~10 min) to grab a map and check trail conditions

### 2:30 PM – Start Hiking

**Trail Options:**

1. **Calico Tanks Trail** (Best scenic views)
   - Distance: 2.2 miles
   - Duration: 1.5 hours
   - Features: Rock scrambling, scenic views

2. **Lost Creek Trail**
   - Features: Short, easy, waterfall

3. **Ice Box Canyon Trail** ⚠️ Requires reservation
   - Distance: 2.6 miles
   - Duration: 2 hours
   - Features: Shady and cool

### 4:30-5:00 PM – Wrap Up & Enjoy Sunset
- If you finish early, drive the Red Rock Canyon Scenic Loop (~30 min) for extra views
- Sunset times:
  - **Winter:** ~5:30 PM
  - **Spring/Fall:** ~7:30 PM
- Great time for photos!

### 6:00 PM – Head Back to Las Vegas
- Drive back to hotel and get ready for evening plans
