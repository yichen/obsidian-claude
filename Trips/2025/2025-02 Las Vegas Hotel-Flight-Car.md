## Things to Do

### Day Trip: Grand Canyon West & Skywalk

**Overview:**
- **Drive Time:** ~2.5 hours from Las Vegas
- **Admission:** $50 (+ $25 for Skywalk)
- **Note:** NOT part of the National Park

**Important Notes:**
- ⚠️ Buy tickets at home and take screenshots/printout (limited network in area, though WiFi available)
- ⚠️ Skywalk line can be 1-hour wait, but view is worth it

**Highlights:**
- **Skywalk** - Located at Eagle Point
- **Eagle Point** - Home of the Skywalk
- **Guano Point** - Better views than Eagle Point, overlooks Colorado River, has hiking trails
- **Colorado River views**

**Sample Itinerary:**
- 7:00 AM: Depart Las Vegas
- 10:00 AM: Arrive at Grand Canyon West
- 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Explore Eagle Point and the Skywalk
- 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM: Lunch at Skywalk Café or Guano Point Café
- 1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Visit Guano Point and Hualapai Ranch
- 3:00 PM: Depart for Las Vegas
- 6:00 PM: Arrive back in Las Vegas

- **Price:** FREE

- **Price:** $20 (book in advance)

### Outdoor Day Trip Options

#### Option 1: Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area
- **Drive Time:** 30 minutes from the Strip
- **Entry Fee:** $15 per vehicle
- **Features:** Stunning red rock formations, hiking trails, scenic drives
- **Best For:** Hiking and photography

#### Option 2: Springs Preserve
- **Size:** 180-acre cultural and historical attraction
- **Admission:** $18.95
- **Features:** Gardens, walking trails, exhibits

#### Option 3: Sunset Park
- **Price:** FREE
- **Features:** Walking, photography, relaxing
- **Note:** Local favorite

---

### Full Day Outdoor Itinerary Options

#### Option A: Hoover Dam Day Trip
**Hoover Dam:**
- **Drive Time:** ~45 minutes
- **Parking:** $10
- **Admission:** FREE to visit
- **Features:** Engineering marvel, stunning photos of dam and Lake Mead

**Valley of Fire State Park:**
- **Drive Time:** ~1 hour from Vegas
- **Entry Fee:** $10 per vehicle
- **Features:** Vibrant red sandstone formations, petroglyphs, hiking trails
- **Best For:** Photography and outdoor exploration

**Seven Magic Mountains:**
- **Drive Time:** 20 minutes south of the Strip
- **Price:** FREE
- **Features:** Instagram-worthy art installation with colorful rock formations
