### Camping Gear & Setup

#### Camp Chairs
✅ **Three people = Need three camp chairs**
- When camping with just me + 2 kids, three chairs needed
- Don't skimp on seating

#### Water Management
📝 **Water Container Strategy:**
- 10-gallon container: Half gone after first evening with 3 people
- **Solution:** Bring 5-gallon portable container as backup
  - Maintains portability
  - Comfortable for 2 nights without refill
- **Note:** 10-gallon probably enough for solo 2-night trip

#### Firewood & Garbage
💡 **Dual-Purpose Cardboard Box:**
- Use cardboard box to transport firewood
- Repurpose as garbage container at camp
- Works great!

#### Power Management
⚠️ **Battery Usage:**
- One 100Ah battery ran out during trip
- Good thing I brought two batteries
- **For 2 nights with 3 people:** Two 100Ah batteries necessary

⚠️ **Starlink Data:**
- Used up entire data plan during trip
- **Action:** Need to upgrade Starlink data plan
- Heavy usage with kids using devices
