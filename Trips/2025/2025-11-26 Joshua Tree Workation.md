### Thu 11/27 — Full Park Exploration (Thanksgiving Day!)

**Morning:**
- **49 Palms Oasis Trail** ⭐ MUST DO
  - 3.1 mi RT, 636 ft gain, moderate, 2-3 hours
  - Climb ridge, descend to hidden palm oasis, remote & scenic
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/49-palms-oasis-trail

- **Hidden Valley**
  - 1 mi loop, easy, enclosed valley surrounded by massive boulders
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/hidden-valley-loop-trail

- **Barker Dam**
  - 1.1 mi loop, easy, 30-45 min
  - Historic dam, rock art, reflections (if water)
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/barker-dam-loop-trail

### Fri 11/28 — Full Hiking Day! (Black Friday - Check for meetings)

- **Ryan Mountain**
  - 3 mi RT, 1,069 ft gain, moderate (steep stone staircase)
  - 360° panoramic views - BEST VIEWS IN ENTIRE PARK
  - Summit elevation: 5,456 ft
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/ryan-mountain-trail

- **Split Rock Loop**
  - 2.5 mi loop, moderate, 275 ft gain, 1.5-2 hours
  - Massive granite boulders, Face Rock, diverse desert scenery
  - **Note:** Replaced Lost Horse Mine (reported closed).
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/split-rock-loop-trail

### Sat 11/29 — Sunrise + Stars

- **Cholla Cactus Garden**
  - 0.25 mi loop, 15-20 min walk
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/cholla-cactus-garden-trail
- **Arch Rock/Heart Rock**
  - Arch Rock: 0.5 mi trail to natural arch formation
- **Wall Street Mill** (easy, 2.2 mi RT, 1.5 hrs)
    - Historic mill ruins, easy walk
