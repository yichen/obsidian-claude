## Trip Report - Critical Incidents & Lessons

### Ruby's Sleepwalking Incident

**What Happened:**
- First night at lodge
- Heard Ruby crying and woke up
- Found door not fully closed
- Discovered Ruby outside in front of another door
- **Assumption:** Ruby got up for bathroom but took wrong door

⚠️ **Safety Concerns:**
- Ruby may have been sleepwalking
- Exited room without waking parents
- Was outside hotel room alone
- Could have gotten lost or gone outside building

**Action Items for Future:**
- [ ] Discuss sleepwalking with pediatrician
- [ ] Consider door alarm for hotel rooms
- [ ] Brief kids on hotel room layout before bed
- [ ] Keep nightlight on in room
- [ ] Consider extra security latch for hotel doors
- [ ] May be related to ADHD medication or stress

## Lessons Learned

### Power & Battery Management

❌ **Anker Battery for Starlink:**
- Only brought large batteries (not portable)
- **Solution:** Put portable Anker battery in Starlink bag permanently
- Keep them together as a kit

❌ **iPad Charging:**
- Only brought large battery (too heavy to carry) and tiny iPhone-only batteries
- iPad couldn't be charged on the go
- **Solution:** Bring mid-size portable battery (10,000-20,000 mAh)
  - Large enough for iPad
  - Small enough to carry comfortably

**Battery Kit Needed:**
1. **Starlink Kit:** Portable Anker battery + Starlink (keep together)
2. **iPad/Phone Kit:** Mid-size battery (10-20K mAh) for portable charging
3. **Car/Basecamp:** Large batteries for stationary use

### Trip Planning

❌ **Miscalculated Driving Distance:**
- Ended up driving too far
- **Solution:** Use Google Maps timeline feature to verify distances
- Check realistic drive times (not just distance)
- Factor in Olympic Peninsula winding roads
