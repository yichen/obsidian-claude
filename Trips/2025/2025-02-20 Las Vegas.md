# Death Valley Day Trip - February 20, 2025

#travel #vegas #2025-02

---

## Overview

**Departure:** 7:00 AM from Las Vegas
**Return:** ~5:30 PM to Las Vegas
**Total Drive Time:** ~5 hours (including stops)
**Route:** US-95 N → NV-160 W → CA-190 W into Death Valley

---

## Itinerary

### 7:00 AM – Depart from Las Vegas
- Grab breakfast and coffee on the way
- Route: US-95 N to NV-160 W, then CA-190 W into Death Valley
- **Drive Time:** ~2 hours

---

### 9:00 AM – Dante's View (Panoramic Overlook)
**Drive Time:** ~20 min detour from main road

**Why Go?**
- Best panoramic view of Death Valley (5,475 ft elevation)
- Overlooks Badwater Basin & Telescope Peak
- Perfect for sunrise or morning photos

---

### 10:00 AM – Zabriskie Point (Iconic Badlands View)
**Drive Time:** ~25 min from Dante's View

**Why Go?**
- Famous golden badlands landscape
- Short walk from parking lot (~5 min)
- Great for morning lighting & photography

---

### 10:30 AM – Furnace Creek Visitor Center (Rest Stop & Info)
**Drive Time:** ~10 min from Zabriskie Point

**Why Go?**
- Restrooms, water refill, and park info
- Grab a park map & check current conditions
- See the hottest recorded temperature display (134°F / 56.7°C in 1913)

---

### 11:00 AM – Badwater Basin (Lowest Point in North America)
**Drive Time:** ~30 min from Furnace Creek

**Why Go?**
- 282 feet below sea level (lowest elevation in North America)
- Walk on the vast salt flats for surreal photos
- Extreme desert landscape with mountain backdrops

---

### 12:00 PM – Artist's Palette (Colorful Rock Formations)
**Drive Time:** ~20 min (along Artist's Drive, a scenic one-way loop)

**Why Go?**
- Vibrant pink, green, purple, and yellow rocks (caused by oxidized minerals)
- Short, easy walk to get closer to the formations
- One of the most unique spots in Death Valley

---

### 1:45 PM – Devil's Golf Course (Salt & Rock Formations)
**Drive Time:** ~15 min from Furnace Creek

**Why Go?**
- Rugged salt formations that look like a giant coral reef
- Strange, jagged terrain—too sharp to walk on!
- Unreal landscape for photos

---

### 2:30 PM – Mesquite Flat Sand Dunes (Classic Desert Dunes)
**Drive Time:** ~40 min from Devil's Golf Course

**Why Go?**
- Rolling golden sand dunes—iconic desert scenery
- Perfect for fun photos & barefoot walks in the sand
- Good chance to spot animal tracks (coyotes, lizards, beetles)

---

### 3:30 PM – Start Driving Back to Las Vegas
**Drive Time:** ~2 hours
- Optional stop in Pahrump for gas or snacks
- Expected arrival in Las Vegas: ~5:30 PM

---

## Essential Items to Bring

- [ ] Plenty of water (at least 1 gallon per person)
- [ ] Sunscreen and hat
- [ ] Snacks and lunch
- [ ] Camera/phone for photos
- [ ] National Park Pass (America the Beautiful)
- [ ] Full tank of gas before leaving Las Vegas
- [ ] Map or downloaded offline maps (cell service limited)

---

## Important Notes

⚠️ **Death Valley can be extremely hot** - bring plenty of water
⚠️ **Limited cell service** - download maps beforehand
⚠️ **Gas up in Las Vegas** - limited fuel options in Death Valley
⚠️ **Check weather conditions** before departing
