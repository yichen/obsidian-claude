# Las Vegas Solo Hiking & Sightseeing Trip

**Dates:** October 22-26, 2025 (Wednesday - Sunday)
**Trip Type:** Solo hiking and sightseeing
**Travel Party:** Solo Traveler
**Home Base:** Las Vegas, NV

## Executive Summary

This 5-day trip focuses on hiking and exploration of the Southwest's most spectacular landscapes. Given the distances involved, I recommend focusing on **Zion and Bryce Canyon only**, saving Joshua Tree for a future California trip. The optimal approach is a **progressive road trip** rather than returning to Las Vegas each night, which would waste 8+ hours of driving time.

Key points:
- **No daily out-and-back from Las Vegas.** One-way drives are long (LAS → Zion ~2.5 hours/160 miles; LAS → Bryce ~4 hours/265 miles).
- **Recommended structure:** Fly into Las Vegas → drive to Springdale (Zion) for 2 nights → drive to Bryce for 2 nights → return to Las Vegas on departure day.

## Feasibility Analysis

### Distance Reality Check from Las Vegas:
- **Zion National Park:** 160 miles (2.5 hours) northeast
- **Bryce Canyon:** 265 miles (4 hours) northeast
- **Joshua Tree:** 225 miles (3.5 hours) southwest

**Critical Issue:** Visiting all three parks requires backtracking through Las Vegas (Zion/Bryce are northeast, Joshua Tree is southwest), adding 450+ miles of unnecessary driving. With only 5 days, this leaves minimal time for actual hiking.

### Recommended Approach: Zion + Bryce Focus

**Why this works better:**
- Natural progression from Las Vegas → Zion → Bryce
- Maximizes hiking time (saves 7+ hours of driving)
- October is peak season for both parks (perfect weather, fall colors)
- Allows for deeper exploration vs. rushed visits

## Detailed Itinerary

### Day 1 - Wednesday, October 22: Travel Day
**Theme:** Arrival and transit to Zion

**Morning:**
- Depart Seattle (SeaTac) on early flight (~7:00 AM)
- Arrive Las Vegas (~9:30 AM)
- Pick up rental car at airport
- Stock up on supplies (water, snacks, lunch)

**Afternoon:**
- **Drive directly to Springdale (Zion gateway town):** 2.5 hours (160 miles) via I-15 N
- Arrive Springdale early afternoon
- Check into hotel (recommendations below)

**Late Afternoon:**
- **Canyon Overlook Trail:** Easy 1-mile hike (1 hour)
  - Short but spectacular views of Zion Canyon
  - Located at east entrance tunnel
  - Perfect warm-up hike
  - **Drive from Springdale:** 15 minutes (10 miles) via SR-9
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/canyon-overlook-trail

**Evening:**
- Return to Springdale for dinner
- Rest and prepare for full hiking day tomorrow

**Total Driving:** ~3 hours
**Hotel:** Springdale, UT (2 nights)

---

### Day 2 - Thursday, October 23: Zion Iconic Hikes
**Theme:** Zion's best trails

**Early Morning (Start by 6:30 AM):**
- **Angels Landing** (if permit secured)
  - **Distance:** 5.4 miles round-trip
  - **Elevation:** 1,488 feet
  - **Duration:** 4-5 hours
  - **Difficulty:** Strenuous with chains section
  - Start early to avoid crowds and heat
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/angels-landing-trail

**Alternative if no Angels Landing permit:**
- **Observation Point via East Mesa Trail**
  - **Distance:** 7 miles round-trip
  - **Elevation:** 655 feet
  - **Duration:** 3-4 hours
  - Better views than Angels Landing, less crowded
  - **Drive from Springdale:** 1 hour via SR-9 E (requires leaving park and re-entering)
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/observation-point-via-east-mesa-trail

**Afternoon:**
- **Emerald Pools Trail** (recovery hike)
  - **Distance:** 3 miles to Upper Pool
  - **Duration:** 2 hours
  - **Difficulty:** Moderate
  - Beautiful waterfalls and pools
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/emerald-pools-trail

**Evening:**
- Sunset at Canyon Overlook Trail (1 mile, 30 minutes)
- Dinner in Springdale

**Total Driving:** Minimal if staying in park / ~2 hours if doing East Mesa
**Work Time:** 2-3 hours in evening if needed (hotel WiFi)

---

### Day 3 - Friday, October 24: The Narrows & Drive to Bryce
**Theme:** Water hike and scenic drive

**Morning:**
- **The Narrows (Bottom-Up)**
  - **Start Time:** 8:00 AM
  - **Distance:** Up to 10 miles (turn around anytime)
  - **Duration:** 4-6 hours
  - **Gear Rental:** Zion Outfitter in Springdale (dry pants, shoes, walking stick)
  - **Water Temperature:** ~55°F in October
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/the-zion-narrows-riverside-walk

**Afternoon:**
- Return gear, grab lunch
- **Drive to Bryce Canyon:** 1.5 hours (85 miles) via US-89 N
- Stop at Red Canyon for photos (15 minutes before Bryce)

**Late Afternoon:**
- Check into Bryce Canyon Lodge or nearby hotel
- **Sunset Point:** Watch sunset (short walk from rim)

**Total Driving:** ~2 hours
**Hotel:** Bryce Canyon area (2 nights)

---

### Day 4 - Saturday, October 25: Bryce Canyon Full Day
**Theme:** Hoodoo exploration

**Early Morning (for best light):**
- **Sunrise at Bryce Point:** 6:30 AM
- Breakfast at Lodge

**Morning/Afternoon Combined Hike:**
- **Navajo/Queens Garden/Peekaboo Loop**
  - **Distance:** 6.4 miles
  - **Elevation:** 1,500 feet
  - **Duration:** 4-5 hours
  - **Difficulty:** Moderate to Strenuous
  - Best way to see the hoodoos up close
  - https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/navajo-queens-garden-and-peekaboo-loop-trail

**Alternative Easier Option:**
- **Queens Garden/Navajo Loop:** 2.9 miles, 2-3 hours

**Late Afternoon:**
- **Drive Scenic Road:** 18 miles to Rainbow Point
- Multiple viewpoints along the way
- **Bristlecone Loop Trail:** Easy 1-mile walk among ancient trees

**Evening:**
- **Stargazing:** Bryce has some of the darkest skies in the US
- Consider ranger-led astronomy program (check schedule)

**Total Driving:** ~1 hour (scenic drive within park)

---

### Day 5 - Sunday, October 26: Return to Las Vegas
**Theme:** Scenic return with optional stops

**Morning:**
- **Mossy Cave Trail:** Easy 0.8-mile hike (30 minutes)
- Located just outside park entrance
- Waterfall and unique hoodoo formations
- https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/utah/mossy-cave-trail

**Drive to Las Vegas:**
- **Route:** US-89 S to I-15 S
- **Distance:** 265 miles (4 hours)
- **Optional Stop:** Coral Pink Sand Dunes State Park (adds 30 minutes)

**Afternoon:**
- Arrive Las Vegas by 2:00 PM
- Return rental car
- Evening flight to Seattle

**Total Driving:** ~4.5 hours
