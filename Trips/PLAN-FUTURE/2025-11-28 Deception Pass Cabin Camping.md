**3:30 PM** - Return to cabin before dark (sunset 4:19 PM)
- Warm up in heated cabin

**5:30 PM** - Dinner in Oak Harbor

**7:00 PM** - **BEST TIDE POOLING** at Rosario Beach

- Bring headlamps and flashlights
