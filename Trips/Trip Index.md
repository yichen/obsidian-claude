### 2024-08 Solo Banff Vacation
- **Type:** Solo hiking trip
- **Dates:** August 10-18, 2024
- **Location:** Banff & Jasper National Parks, Alberta, Canada
- **Route:** Sammamish → Kamloops → Revelstoke → Golden → Canmore/Banff → Kelowna → Sammamish
- **Highlights:** Lake Louise, Moraine Lake, Icefields Parkway, mountain hikes
- **File:** [2024/2024-08  Solo Vacation at Banff.md](2024/2024-08%20%20Solo%20Vacation%20at%20Banff.md)
- **Key Lessons:**
  - Should have brought folding camp chair
  - Should have brought folding hiking poles for wet/slippery trails (Sulphur Mountain)
  - Roof top box problematic for Airbnb garage parking
  - Should have brought carbohydrate pills (got dizzy hiking in fasted state)
  - **Over-packed clothes:** 3 running shirts + 3 hiking shirts + 3 everyday shirts = too much. Stick to 3 everyday + 3 hiking shirts only
  - Should have brought rain pants (bought umbrella at Moraine Lake)
  - Canmore Elevation Place has great kids activities
  - Huge dinosaur museum east of Banff in Alberta (for future family trip)
  - Revelstoke has many kids-friendly attractions (gondola, pipe mountain coaster, hot springs, 3 Valley Ghost Town)
- **Tags:** #banff #solo-trip #canada

## Usage Notes

### For Trip Planning:
1. **Check similar past trips** for lessons learned and packing insights
2. **Reference previous visits** to same destination to avoid repeating activities
3. **Review seasonal timing** - some destinations visited in different seasons

### For Kauai Trips Specifically:
- July/August 2025: Solo workation, hiked Sleeping Giant East, Kuilau Ridge, Waipoo Falls, Awa'awapuhi, Maha'ulepu Heritage
- October 2025: Solo workation, hiked Wai Koa Loop, Ho'opi'i Falls, Nu'alolo, Kalalau to Hanakapi'ai Falls

### For China Trips Specifically:
- **2023-06:** Family trip, returned June 8. Jet lag recovery ~5 days. Very hot (90°F+), Nov/Dec better per locals
- **2025-12:** Solo trip (Shenzhen family visit + Lijiang). Yulong Snow Mountain (4,680m), Tiger Leaping Gorge hike
- **Key Lessons:**
  - Jet lag: Consistently takes ~5 days to adjust both directions
  - Trip.com essential for domestic flights/hotels
  - RedNote (小红书) critical for travel planning in China
  - Bring soft-shell bag for domestic travel (not hard suitcase)
  - Domestic flights require 3C-certified power banks
  - Bring Kindle - no WiFi on some domestic airlines (Shenzhen/Tibet Airlines)
  - Can ship gifts home via SF Express (顺丰) instead of packing in luggage

### Key Lessons Across Multiple Trips:
- **Packing:** Make AND follow packing list (learned from Oregon Coast trip)
- **Weather prep:** Always bring layers for October trips (North Cascades)
- **Traffic:** Avoid Friday afternoon southbound I-5 (Silver Falls, Great Wolf Lodge)
- **Solo hiking:** Trekking poles essential, don't overpack clothes (Banff)
- **First night camping:** Always hardest, but recovery is good (Silver Falls)
