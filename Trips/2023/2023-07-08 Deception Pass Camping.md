## Behavioral Strategy for Kids

**What Worked:**
- Set clear goals/rewards for good behavior
- Laurence: Earned camera for good behavior
- Ruby: Earned bandits for good behavior
- **Key mindset:** Prioritize kids' needs over my own comfort
  - Mentally prepared for no personal comfort
  - Assume failure of personal comfort
  - This approach worked well and reduced stress

## Lessons Learned

### Gear & Equipment
✅ **Large garbage bag** - Place by camp garbage bin so you don't forget
✅ **In-car pee bag** - Very helpful for long drives
✅ **Diaper for Laurence in car** - Essential for travel
✅ **Two burners needed** - One burner insufficient when cooking separate meals (kid food + adult food)
✅ **Larger spoon** - Need bigger spoon for pot set
✅ **Trailer cover** - Essential! Bags blow away on highway without it
✅ **Large hard water container** - Soft container falls off bench too easily (even though small size has enough water)
✅ **Folding table** - Need one to use inside tent

### Camping Strategy
✅ **Campnab tool works well** - Found spot by checking Friday for Saturday night availability (most cancellations happen day before)
✅ **Mental preparation is key** - "Nothing will stress me out, prioritize kids' needs over mine"
  - Comes from mindset of assuming failure (of personal comfort)
  - This approach worked very well

### Behavioral Management
✅ **Goal-setting for kids** - Clear rewards system works
  - Laurence worked toward earning camera
  - Ruby worked toward earning bandits
  - Kids behaved much better with clear incentives

### Sleep Quality
❓ **Didn't sleep as well compared to bell tent** - Why?
  - Need to investigate cause
  - Consider bell tent for future trips if sleep quality is priority
