# Cougar Rock Campground

**Hike**
- Just across the road from Cougar Rock Campground is the **Wonderland Trail**. Enjoy an incredible mountain view and walk along the Nisqually and Paradise Rivers on the easy to moderate hike. You can hike up 1.1 miles to the **Carter and Madcap Falls**. Hike an additional 1.5 miles past Carter Falls to see **Narada Falls**. Or you can hike down 1.5 miles to go to the Longmire Area. Other trails that are close to you here are the Trail of Shadows, Rampart Ridge, Comet Falls, and Paradise Area Trails. Narada Falls, as well as **Christine Falls**, can also be viewed from short walks from pullouts along the main road to Paradise.
- Recent trip reports recommends kids friendly  [Silver Falls trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/washington/silver-falls-trail)
	- 4 mile loop trail near Paradise Inn. 
- [Bench and Snow Lake Trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/washington/bench-and-snow-lakes-trail)
	- 2.2 miles out-and-back trail, just about 1 hour to finish, so this is a short trail
- [Narada Falls Trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/washington/narada-falls-trail)
	- 2.4 miles out-and-back, close to Paradise Inn
- 

**Sunset**
- On the west side of the park we have many wonderful sunset locations. You can go to the historic **Longmire Suspension Bridge** to see the alpenglow on the mountain. If you are willing to drive a little further you can view the sunset at **Reflection Lake,** from **Ricksecker Point,** or in Paradise. These areas are also the best to safely stargaze

# Ohanapecosh Campground

- Grove of the Patriarchs  (closed, bridge is out)
	- 6 minutes of drive from the campground
- Naches Peak Loop Trail
	- 28 minutes of drive from the campground
	- 3.3 miles roundtrip, kids friendly
	- there are snows.
- Skyline Trail
	- 45 minutes of drive from the campground
	- 5.6 miles round trip
- Narada Falls Trail
	- 40 minutes drive from the campground (close to Skyline Loop Trail)
- Bench and Snow Lake Trail
	- 34 minutes of drive from the campground
- Christine Falls
	- 48 minutes of drive from the campground (5 minutes of drive from Cougar Rock Campground)
- Ohanapecosh is still one of the best campground....a lot of sites with little creeks, a hike to silver falls and a dip in the blue hole glacial water. Plus the small quaint hot spring, and little "museum". Bring your bikes! No showers, but clean bathroom. No hook ups.
