### International Trips (China)
- **School break + Chinese New Year = ALWAYS go to China.** Take kids if you have custody, go solo if you don't. One-week breaks are viable — other families (Chinese and American) routinely do this. CNY is ideal because China is in holiday mode (family-focused, less to "do" = less FOMO about trip length). Regretted not doing this for 2026 mid-winter break (CNY was Mon Feb 16). **Plan 3 months ahead when school calendar is released.**
- Allow 5 days for jet lag recovery (consistent pattern) [[2023/2023-06 China trip|China 2023]] [[2025/2025-12-17 Solo Trip to China/2025-12-17 China Trip Report|China 2025]]

- RedNote (小红书) essential for travel planning in China [[2025/2025-12-17 Solo Trip to China/2025-12-17 China Trip Report|China 2025]]
