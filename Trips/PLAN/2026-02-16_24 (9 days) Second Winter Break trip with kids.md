## Brainstorming Options (9 Days in February)

Since you have a full 9 days, this is perfect for a "fly-away" trip to escape the PNW winter.

### Option 1: The "Theme Park & Sun" Trip (San Diego, CA)
*   **Vibe:** Fun, educational, and sunny (60s-70s°F).
*   **Why:** Perfect age (8yo) for **Legoland**.
*   **Itinerary Sketch:**
    *   Days 1-3: **Legoland California** (Carlsbad). Stay at the Legoland Hotel (kids love it).
    *   Days 4-6: **San Diego Zoo** & **Safari Park** (Escondido). World-class animals.
    *   Days 7-9: **Coronado Beach** or **La Jolla** (Seals/Sea Lions) + **USS Midway Museum**.
*   **Pros:** Direct flights, very kid-centric, easy logistics.
*   **Cons:** Can be pricey (theme park tickets).

### Option 2: The "Volcano & Adventure" Trip (Big Island, Hawaii)
*   **Vibe:** Nature, science, and warm ocean.
*   **Why:** You've done Kauai (Garden Isle); Big Island offers **Volcanoes National Park** and calm beaches for kids.
*   **Itinerary Sketch:**
    *   Days 1-4: **Kona side**. Snorkeling at Kahalu’u Beach (very safe/shallow), Seahorse Farm.
    *   Days 5-7: **Volcano Village**. Explore lava tubes, craters, and steam vents in the National Park.
    *   Days 8-9: **Hilo side** (Waterfalls) or relax at resort.
*   **Pros:** Educational (geology), warm weather, direct flights to Kona.
*   **Cons:** Lots of driving (Big Island is huge).

### Option 3: The "Red Rocks" Road Trip (Arizona)
*   **Vibe:** Hiking, scenery, and desert exploration.
*   **Why:** February is cool but sunny, perfect for hiking without the scorching heat.
*   **Itinerary Sketch:**
    *   Days 1-3: **Phoenix/Scottsdale**. Musical Instrument Museum, Desert Botanical Garden, easy hikes (Papago Park).
    *   Days 4-6: **Sedona**. Pink Jeep Tour (kids love the bumpy ride), Slide Rock State Park (no sliding in Feb, but pretty), hiking Bell Rock.
    *   Days 7-9: **Grand Canyon (South Rim)**. It might have snow on the rim (beautiful!), but you can walk the paved trail. Or head back to Phoenix for a resort pool day.
*   **Pros:** Stunning scenery, manageable hikes, distinct from PNW.
*   **Cons:** Grand Canyon can be very cold/snowy in Feb.

### Option 4: The "International Culture" Trip (Mexico - Cabo or Puerto Vallarta)
*   **Vibe:** Resort relaxation, pools, tacos, and culture.
*   **Why:** Guaranteed warmth and direct flights.
*   **Itinerary Sketch:**
    *   Stay at a family-friendly all-inclusive or a nice Airbnb.
    *   Whale watching (peak season in Feb!).
    *   Snorkeling tours.
    *   Relaxing by the pool.
*   **Pros:** Real relaxation, great food, warm ocean.
*   **Cons:** Passports required (check expiration!), drinking water caution.
