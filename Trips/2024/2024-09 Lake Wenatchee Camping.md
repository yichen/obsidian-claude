## Packing List

### Clothing
- [ ] Quick setup tent
- [ ] Rain jacket (in case of rain)
- [ ] Thick hiking pants (in case of rain)
- [ ] Extra warm clothes for cold weather (Enchantment area: high 40s, low 20s)

- [ ] NO cotton shirts (switch to synthetic/wool)

## Lessons Learned

### Campground Strategy
- Several campgrounds available, including 8 Mile Campground between Leavenworth and trailhead
- Old Mill Cafe has free WiFi and lots of space - can work here during the day
- This enables longer stays including weekdays

### Clothing
- ❌ Forgot to change out of cotton shirts - NOT good for hiking
- ✅ Toe socks are great for hiking - buy another pair
- ✅ Pack synthetic/wool layers instead of cotton

### Food Strategy
- ✅ Trader Joe's trail mix works well for lunch
- ✅ Early dinner at restaurant is a good option
- ✅ Protein bars for snacking between meals
- ⚠️ May not need to bring dinner supplies (Leavenworth is close)

### Saturday Morning
- Pack tent before hike in case of early return home

### Weather Planning
- Check mountain-forecast.com for Enchantment Peak for latest conditions
- Enchantment area can be very cold (high 40s, low 20s) - pack extra layers
- Consider weather when choosing between Saturday/Sunday hikes
