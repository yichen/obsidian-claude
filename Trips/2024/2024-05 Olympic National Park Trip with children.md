	- Lake Quinault as a break spot. I personally love the area and it's not as busy as Hoh. If you take North Shore Road you can picnic at July Creek. Then drive down to the ranger station parking lot to do a quick hike on Maple Glade and Kestner Homestead trail. That will give you a rainforest experience and see a cool former ranch as well. Get the NPS app and download Rainier and Olympic for offline use.
	- Highly recommend the Quinault Rain Forest Nature Trail, it's a short, easy, scenic trail, much less crowded than the Hoh. Exit off of 101 onto 93400 South Shore Rd, trailhead is approx 1.5 miles on the right, if you reach the lodge, you will have driven past the trailhead. Also, approx a mile past the lodge, you can see the World's Largest Sitka Spruce.
