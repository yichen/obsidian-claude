# Olympic National Park with Kids — Saturday, March 21

## 09:00 PST — Day 2: Tree of Life, Beach 4 & Sol Duc Hot Springs

Decided to head out earlier than usual, hoping to return to the lodge earlier so the kids could play arcade. Left around 9AM.

**Plan for the day:** Tree of Life → Beach 4 (tidal pools) → optionally Rialto Beach → Sol Duc Hot Springs (high priority — first day of the season).

Ruby was excited to take photos on this trip and shot a lot at the Tree of Life and Beach 4. Both kids had been here before and remembered. Nice to see them with that sense of familiarity with the park.

I decided to skip Rialto Beach and head straight to Sol Duc Hot Springs to make sure we got there. Forgot how long the drive is. By the time we reached Forks to charge the Tesla, it was already lunch time, so we ate pizza across the street from the supercharger.

Laurence noticed the Indian restaurant we ate at during our last trip — just the two of us back then. I was surprised he remembered, or maybe my memory is just getting worse.

We stayed at the hot springs for about an hour, then bought a stuffie for each kid at the gift shop. By the time we were back in Forks charging, it was 5:45PM by the time we'd get back to the lodge — much later than I anticipated. The arcade plan didn't pan out.
