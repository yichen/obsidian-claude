# Olympic National Park with Kids — Sunday, March 22

## 08:00 PST — Food & Cooking Reflection

Brought a compact cooking pot that works in hotel/lodge rooms. It worked great here — we made instant noodles for dinner last night instead of eating out. Saved a significant amount of money: we didn't eat at the lodge restaurant at all this trip, and we skipped the nearby restaurant we overspent at during our last Olympic Peninsula trip.

Overall food spending this trip was noticeably lower. The cooking pot is a keeper for future lodge trips.
