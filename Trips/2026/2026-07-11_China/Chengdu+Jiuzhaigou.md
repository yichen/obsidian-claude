## 7-Day Itinerary: Pandas & Blue Water

### Day 1: Arrival & Hot Pot Survival
*   **Location:** Chengdu
*   **Theme:** Jet Lag Recovery & Food
*   **Logistics:** Arrive Chengdu Tianfu (TFU) or Shuangliu (CTU). Transfer to hotel.
*   **Activity:**
    *   Check into hotel (Suggest **The Ritz-Carlton** or **St. Regis** - safe bets for English service/concierge).
    *   **Dinner:** Mild Tomato Broth Hot Pot (Haidilao is kid-safe/entertaining).
    *   **Sleep:** Early.

### Day 2: The Panda Mission
*   **Location:** Chengdu
*   **Theme:** Giant Pandas! (The trip hook)
*   **Logistics:** Pre-book tickets!
*   **Morning (7:30 AM):** **Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding**.
    *   *Why Early?* Pandas sleep by 9-10 AM when it gets hot. Go straight to the "Moon Delivery Room" for babies.
    *   *Lunch:* Inside the base or "Bamboo" themed restaurant.
*   **Afternoon:** Nap / Hotel Pool (Heat avoidance).
*   **Evening:** **Jinli Ancient Street** or **Kuanzhai Alley** (Ear cleaning, face changing show - kids might find face changing scary or cool, judge accordingly).

### Day 3: Escape to the Cool Mountains (High-Speed Rail)
*   **Location:** Chengdu -> Jiuzhaigou
*   **Theme:** Journey to the West
*   **Logistics:** Train C/G series from **Chengdu East** to **Huanglongjiuzhai Station**.
    *   *Time:* ~1.5 - 2 hours.
*   **Activity:**
    *   **Huanglong Scenic Area:** The train station is near Huanglong. Take the cable car UP.
    *   *Highlight:* Multi-colored calcium pools. 
    *   *Altitude Warning:* Huanglong is high (~3500m). Buy oxygen canisters for kids just in case. Walk SLOW.
*   **Transfer:** Hired car/bus to Jiuzhaigou Hotel (e.g., **Hilton Jiuzhaigou Resort** or **Rissai Valley**).

### Day 4: The Fairyland (Jiuzhaigou)
*   **Location:** Jiuzhaigou National Park
*   **Theme:** "Avatar" / "Disney" Water
*   **Weather:** Expect ~20°C (perfect summer weather). Rain is possible (bring rain jackets).
*   **Logistics:** Park Bus (hop-on hop-off).
*   **Highlights:**
    *   **Five Flower Lake:** The most colorful water.
    *   **Long Lake:** Highest altitude, deep blue.
    *   **Nuorilang Waterfall:** Massive, iconic.
    *   *Kid Note:* It's a lot of walking/bus riding. Bring snacks.

### Day 5: Relax or Repeat -> Return
*   **Location:** Jiuzhaigou -> Chengdu
*   **Theme:** Leisure
*   **Option A:** Sleep in, enjoy hotel resort/pool (if remaining in Jiuzhaigou).
*   **Option B:** Short hike in a non-core area (e.g., Zharu Valley).
*   **Afternoon:** High-Speed Train back to Chengdu.
*   **Dinner:** Chengdu Street Snacks (Dan Dan Noodles - ask for non-spicy "Bu La").

### Day 6: Ancient Engineering or "Aliens"
*   **Location:** Chengdu Outskirts
*   **Option A (Nature/Water):** **Dujiangyan Irrigation System**. 2200 years old, still works. Panda Valley is nearby (less crowded than Chengdu Base).
*   **Option B (Cool Masks):** **Sanxingdui Museum**. "Alien-looking" bronze masks. Very distinct from typical Chinese history. Great for 8yo imagination.
*   **Evening:** Relaxing tea house in People's Park. Locals dancing (kids can join).

### Day 7: Departure
*   **Activity:** Last minute souvenir shopping (Panda plushies).
*   **Flight:** Depart.
