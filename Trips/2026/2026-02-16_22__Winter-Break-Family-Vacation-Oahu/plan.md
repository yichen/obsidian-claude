## ⚠️ CRITICAL: Required Reservations & Permits

> **🔴 BIG RED WARNING: Book these BEFORE your trip!**

### 1. Pearl Harbor / USS Arizona Memorial
- **Book:** Up to **8 weeks (56 days) in advance** via [Recreation.gov](https://www.recreation.gov/ticket/facility/233338)
- **Cost:** $1 service fee per ticket (memorial is free)
- **IMPORTANT:** Tickets sell out in minutes! Create account beforehand. All visitors need tickets including children.
- **No bags allowed** - use baggage storage at visitor center
- **Parking:** $7/day (pay on-site)

### 2. Hanauma Bay Nature Preserve
- **Book:** Exactly **2 days before** your visit at 7:00 AM HST via [Hanauma Bay Reservations](https://web5.hnl.info/pros-ticketing/event/hbay-online-reservation)
- **Cost:** $25/person (age 13+), children 12 and under FREE
- **Hours:** Wed-Sun only, 6:45 AM - 4:00 PM (last entry 1:30 PM)
- **CLOSED:** Monday & Tuesday
- **Alternative:** Book shuttle package through [Roberts Hawaii](https://www.robertshawaii.com/) up to 1 month in advance

### 3. Diamond Head State Monument
- **Book:** Up to **30 days in advance** via [Go Hawaii State Parks](https://gostateparks.hawaii.gov/diamondhead)
- **Cost:** $5/person entry + $10 parking (children 3 and under free)
- **Hours:** 6:00 AM - 4:30 PM (gates locked at 6:00 PM)
- **Arrive within 30 minutes** of reservation time or be denied entry

### Pass/Permit Checklist
- [ ] Pearl Harbor tickets (book 8 weeks ahead - **Book by ~Dec 22, 2025!**)
- [ ] Diamond Head reservation (book 30 days ahead - **Book by Jan 17, 2026**)
- [ ] Hanauma Bay reservation (book 2 days ahead - **Book morning of Feb 17 for Feb 19**)

## 🌡️ Weather & Driving Conditions

### February Weather on Oahu
- **Daytime:** 77-79°F (25-26°C)
- **Nighttime:** 64-72°F (18-22°C)
- **Ocean Temperature:** 75°F (24°C) - great for swimming!
- **Rainfall:** Moderate (~3.6 inches over ~5 days) - brief showers possible
- **Sunshine:** ~7 hours/day
- **Humidity:** 68-74%

### 🐋 BONUS: Whale Watching Season!
February is peak whale watching season (December-May). Humpback whales migrate to Hawaii's warm waters. Best viewing spots:
- Makapuu Lighthouse Trail
- North Shore
- Any elevated coastal viewpoint

### Driving Conditions Warning ⚠️
- **Oahu roads:** Generally excellent year-round
- **North Shore:** Can be crowded on weekends - go early
- **H-1 Freeway:** Traffic congestion during rush hours (7-9 AM, 4-6 PM)
- **Rain:** Brief tropical showers can reduce visibility - use caution

### Day 2 - Tuesday, Feb 17: Pearl Harbor & Honolulu

**Theme:** History & Education (Best for Pearl Harbor - quieter weekday)

**Morning:**
- **Pearl Harbor National Memorial**
  - **Drive from hotel:** 25-30 minutes (8 miles) via H-1 West
  - [Pearl Harbor Official Site](https://pearlharbor.org/)
  - [YouTube: Pearl Harbor Tour Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bXYtJMJNmTo) (2024)
  - Arrive 1 hour before ticket time
  - USS Arizona Memorial program (~75 min total)
  - Allow 3-4 hours total for the visit

**Recommended Add-ons (if time permits):**
- Battleship Missouri Memorial ($35 adults, $18 kids 4-12)
- Pacific Aviation Museum

**Afternoon:**
- Lunch at Nico's Pier 38 (fresh poke, near Pearl Harbor)
- **Bishop Museum** (optional, great for kids interested in Hawaii history)
  - **Drive from Pearl Harbor:** 10 minutes (3 miles)
  - [Bishop Museum Website](https://www.bishopmuseum.org/)
  - [YouTube: Bishop Museum Tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qf_8fSkiGVM)
  - Science Adventure Center - interactive exhibits for kids

**Evening:**
- Return to Waikiki
- Dinner & beach sunset

**Total Driving:** ~1 hour

---

### Day 3 - Wednesday, Feb 18: Diamond Head & East Oahu

**Theme:** Iconic Hike & Scenic East Coast

**Early Morning (Beat the heat!):**
- **Diamond Head State Monument**
  - **Drive from hotel:** 10-15 minutes (2 miles)
  - [Diamond Head Official Site](https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dsp/parks/oahu/diamond-head-state-monument/)
  - [AllTrails: Diamond Head](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/hawaii/oahu/diamond-head-leahi-summit-trail)
  - [YouTube: Diamond Head Hike with Kids](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q_3BoQ7VdJE) (2024)
  - **Hike Details:** 1.8 miles roundtrip, 560 ft elevation gain
  - **Duration:** 1.5-2 hours
  - **Kid-friendly:** Yes, but steep sections with stairs (99 steps + 76 steps)
  - Book 6:00-7:00 AM slot to avoid crowds and heat

**Late Morning:**
- Return to hotel, cool off at pool or beach
- Lunch in Waikiki

**Afternoon:**
- **Makapuu Lighthouse Trail** (Whale watching!)
  - **Drive from hotel:** 25 minutes (12 miles) via Kalanianaole Hwy
  - [AllTrails: Makapuu Point Lighthouse Trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/hawaii/oahu/makapu-u-point-lighthouse-trail)
  - [YouTube: Makapuu Lighthouse Trail](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_zF7s5H-G3c)
  - **Hike Details:** 2.5 miles roundtrip, paved, stroller-friendly
  - **Duration:** 1-1.5 hours
  - **February bonus:** Prime whale watching spot! 🐋
  - Free parking, no reservation needed

**Optional Stop:**
- Halona Blowhole Lookout (on the way back, quick photo stop)
- Sandy Beach (watch bodysurfers - not safe for swimming)

**Evening:**
- Return to Waikiki for dinner

**Total Driving:** ~1 hour

---

### Day 4 - Thursday, Feb 19: Hanauma Bay Snorkeling

**Theme:** World-Class Snorkeling (MUST book 2 days ahead!)

> ⚠️ **REMINDER:** Book Hanauma Bay tickets on **Tuesday, Feb 17 at 7:00 AM HST**

**Morning:**
- **Hanauma Bay Nature Preserve**
  - **Drive from hotel:** 25-30 minutes (10 miles)
  - [Hanauma Bay Official Site](https://www.honolulu.gov/dpr/hanauma-bay-nature-preserve/)
  - [YouTube: Hanauma Bay Snorkeling Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HF8bX9u5YYQ)
  - Book early morning slot (opens 6:45 AM) - less crowded, calmer water
  - Watch required 9-minute educational video
  - **Snorkeling:** Incredible coral reef, sea turtles, tropical fish
  - Rent snorkel gear on-site or bring your own
  - **Duration:** 3-4 hours

**Afternoon:**
- **Koko Head Crater Lookout** (quick stop, beautiful views)
- Return to hotel for rest

**Late Afternoon:**
- **Honolulu Zoo** (Perfect for tired kids!)
  - **Walk from hotel:** 10 minutes
  - [Honolulu Zoo Website](https://www.honoluluzoo.org/)
  - 42 acres, 900+ animals including Hawaiian Nene birds
  - **Hours:** 10 AM - 4 PM
  - **Cost:** $25 adults, $15 kids (3-12)

**Evening:**
- Dinner at Kapiolani Park area
- Optional: Waikiki evening stroll

**Total Driving:** ~1 hour

---

### Day 5 - Friday, Feb 20: North Shore Adventure

**Theme:** Surfing Culture, Sea Turtles & Shrimp Trucks

**Morning (Leave early - avoid traffic!):**
- Depart by 8:00 AM
- **Dole Plantation** (quick stop)
  - **Drive from hotel:** 40 minutes (25 miles) via H-2 North
  - [Dole Plantation Website](https://doleplantation.com/)
  - [YouTube: Dole Plantation Tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xk_1O0iN3iQ)
  - Pineapple Garden Maze (world's largest!)
  - Dole Whip (iconic pineapple soft serve)
  - **Duration:** 45 min - 1 hour

**Late Morning:**
- **Haleiwa Town** (Historic surf town)
  - **Drive from Dole:** 15 minutes (8 miles)
  - Explore surf shops, shave ice, art galleries
  - **Lunch:** Giovanni's Shrimp Truck or Romy's Shrimp (North Shore famous!)
  - [YouTube: North Shore Food Tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mYiS8_o-ozI)

**Afternoon:**
- **Laniakea Beach (Turtle Beach)** 🐢
  - **Drive from Haleiwa:** 5 minutes (2 miles)
  - Green sea turtles rest on the beach - great for kids!
  - **Rules:** Stay 10 feet away from turtles, no touching
  - FREE, no reservation needed

- **Waimea Bay Beach Park**
  - **Drive from Laniakea:** 5 minutes (3 miles)
  - Watch big wave surfers (winter is big wave season!)
  - Beach time, jumping rock (if conditions allow)
  - [YouTube: Waimea Bay Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Gk7sQA1IeYE)

**Optional:**
- **Sunset Beach** - famous surfing spot
- **Shark's Cove** - tide pools & snorkeling (if calm)

**Evening:**
- Return to Waikiki via H-2/H-1 (~1 hour)
- Dinner in Waikiki

**Total Driving:** ~2.5 hours

---

### Day 6 - Saturday, Feb 21: Kualoa Ranch & Windward Coast

**Theme:** Movie Magic & Scenic Beauty

**Morning:**
- **Kualoa Ranch**
  - **Drive from hotel:** 40-45 minutes (25 miles) via H-1/Pali Hwy
  - [Kualoa Ranch Website](https://www.kualoa.com/)
  - [YouTube: Kualoa Ranch Tour](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TgfKx8WYJFA) (2024)

  **Tour Options (book in advance!):**
  - **Hollywood Movie Sites Tour** (1.5 hrs, ~$55/person) - See Jurassic Park, Lost, 50 First Dates filming locations
  - **Jungle Expedition Tour** (1.5 hrs, ~$55/person) - WWII bunkers, rainforest
  - **Ocean Voyage Tour** (1.5 hrs, ~$55/person) - Boat tour, fish feeding

  **Duration:** 2-3 hours depending on tours chosen

**Midday:**
- Drive scenic Kamehameha Highway (East Coast)
- **Byodo-In Temple**
  - **Drive from Kualoa:** 15 minutes (6 miles)
  - [Byodo-In Temple](https://www.byodo-in.com/)
  - Beautiful Japanese temple replica in Valley of the Temples
  - Peaceful gardens, koi ponds
  - **Cost:** $5 adults, $2 kids
  - **Duration:** 30-45 min

**Afternoon:**
- **Lanikai Beach** (One of Hawaii's most beautiful beaches!)
  - **Drive from Byodo-In:** 20 minutes (10 miles)
  - Calm, crystal-clear water perfect for kids
  - [YouTube: Lanikai Beach Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KhDCqJZnpO4)
  - Limited parking - arrive early afternoon

- **Alternative:** Kailua Beach Park (more parking, same gorgeous beach)

**Evening:**
- Return to Waikiki via Pali Highway (30 min)
- **Farewell Dinner:** Consider a nice restaurant in Waikiki
  - Roy's Waikiki (Hawaiian fusion)
  - Hula Grill (beachfront dining)
  - Cheesecake Factory (family crowd-pleaser)

**Total Driving:** ~2 hours
