## Destinations That Can Be Combined

### PCH Coastal Route (CA-1 South)
These destinations are along the same coastal highway and can be visited in one trip:

**Hotel → Newport Beach → Crystal Cove → Laguna Beach → Hotel**

| Stop | From Previous | Total from Hotel |
|------|---------------|------------------|
| Newport Beach / Balboa Island | — | 8 miles (15-20 min) |
| Crystal Cove State Park | 4 miles (10 min) | 12 miles |
| Laguna Beach (Heisler Park) | 3 miles (10 min) | 15 miles |
| Crescent Bay Point Park | 1 mile (5 min) | 16 miles |
| Victoria Beach | same area | 15 miles |

**Recommendation:** Visit Crystal Cove in the morning, then continue south to Laguna Beach for sunset. Or start at Newport/Balboa, drive south to Crystal Cove, then Laguna Beach—all on one route.

---

### Inland Irvine Area
These destinations are clustered in the Irvine area (inland from hotel):

- Irvine Spectrum Center
- Turtle Rock Trail
- Irvine Farmers Market (Great Park)

**Recommendation:** Combine Turtle Rock Trail hike with a visit to Irvine Spectrum Center afterward—both are in the same area.

---

### Costa Mesa Arts District
These are next to each other in Costa Mesa:

- Segerstrom Center for the Arts
- Orange County Museum of Art

**Recommendation:** Perfect rainy day combo—both are within walking distance of each other.

---

## Efficient Day Plans

### Coastal Day
Newport Beach → Crystal Cove → Laguna Beach
- Total driving: ~45 min (not counting return)
- All on PCH, natural progression south

### Hiking + Exploration Day
Turtle Rock Trail → Irvine Spectrum Center
- Both in Irvine, 5 min apart
- Hike in morning, explore Spectrum after

### Culture Day (Rainy Weather)
Costa Mesa: Segerstrom Center + OC Museum of Art
- Walking distance apart
- Indoor activities for bad weather
