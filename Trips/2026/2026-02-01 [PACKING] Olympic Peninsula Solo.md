# 2026-02-01 [PACKING] Olympic Peninsula Solo

Custom checklist based on **Solo Hiking** and **Winter Coast** conditions.

## 🥾 Hiking & Outerwear
- [ ] **Waterproof Hiking Boots** (essential for muddy rainforest trails)
- [ ] **Rain Pants & Rain Jacket** (learned from Banff: don't rely on an umbrella)
- [ ] **Trekking Poles** (essential for slippery/wet descents)
- [ ] **Synthetic Base Layers** (avoid cotton; use wool or polyester)
- [ ] **Wool Socks** (Extra pairs! Feet will get wet)
- [ ] **Heated Jacket** (from Costco - great for chilly beach sunsets)

## 📡 Tech & Work-from-Anywhere
- [ ] **Starlink Mini** (Kalaloch/Ruby Beach have very poor cell signal)
- [ ] **Anker Power Bank** (for charging phone/Starlink on the go)
- [ ] **Camera + Tripod** (essential for Ruby Beach photography)
- [ ] **Kindle** (evening reading at the lodge)

## 🧼 Personal & Wellness
- [ ] **Lodge Slippers** (Lodge floors can be cold; lodge doesn't provide them)
- [ ] **Carbohydrate/Electrolyte Pills** (Prevents dizziness during morning hikes)
- [ ] **Toiletries Kit** (Don't forget nasal spray and eye drops)
- [ ] **Swimsuit** (The lodge has an indoor pool/hot tub!)

## 🚗 Car Essentials
- [ ] **Discover Pass / America the Beautiful Pass**
- [ ] **Emergency Roadside Kit**
- [ ] **Water Bottles** (filled)
- [ ] **Grab-and-go Snacks** (Jerky, nuts, fruit)

> [!TIP]
> **Lesson from Kauai:** Your insomnia improved with active hiking. Make sure to actually hit the Quinault trail even if it's raining!
