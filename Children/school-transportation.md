## Procedures

- **Late arrival:** Must sign in at the office and get an "admit slip" for class
- **Early pickup:** Must sign out at the office; notify teacher in advance
- **Transportation changes:** Notify teacher in advance of any changes

## Pickup Location

Wait at the front of the school by dismissal time.
