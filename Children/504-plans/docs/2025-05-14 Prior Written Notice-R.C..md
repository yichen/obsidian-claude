## Description of the Proposed or Refused Action

We are proposing to initiate a 504 evaluation for Ruby at this time.

---

## The Reason We Are Proposing or Refusing to Take Action

We are proposing this action as Ruby was recently diagnosed with the following conditions:

- 314.01 Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, combined type, by history (ICD-10: F90.2) with Sensory Processing Issues (SPD) (ICD-10: F84.0)
- 315.00 Specific Learning Disorder in Reading Comprehension (Dyslexia) (ICD-10: F81.0)
- 300.02 Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD) (ICD-10: F41.1)

The guidance team believes that Ruby may benefit from the provision of certain targeted accommodations in the classroom to support her progress.

---

## Any Other Factors that are Relevant to the Action

Ruby is a multi-language learner and receives MLL services.

There were additional concerns related to manifestations of anxiety at school (e.g., biting at fingernails) that were noted by parent and confirmed by teacher. The school psychologist indicated that additional resources for these issues would be shared with parent(s).

---

## Description of any Other Options Considered and Rejected

A special education evaluation was discussed.

### The Reason(s) We Rejected those Options

This option was presently rejected as Ruby is making both academic and social/behavioral progress, per multiple measures including:

- iREADY diagnostics
- SAEBRS screener data
- MLL services
- Grade report data
- Teacher observations

It was noted that, should rate of progress decrease, or an increase in problematic behaviors become present, the guidance team may be reconvened to discuss the sufficiency of a 504 plan and whether a special education evaluation may be warranted.
