## The Reason We Are Proposing or Refusing to Take Action

We are proposing this action as Laurence was recently diagnosed with the following conditions:

- **314.01** Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, combined type, by history (ICD-10: F90.2) with Sensory Processing Issues (SPD) (ICD-10: F84.0)
- **315.00** Specific Learning Disorder in Reading Comprehension (Dyslexia) (ICD-10: F81.0)
- **300.02** Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD) (ICD-10: F41.1)

The guidance team believes that Laurence may benefit from the provision of certain targeted accommodations in the classroom to support his progress.

---

## Any Other Factors that are Relevant to the Action

Laurence is a multi-language learner receives MLL services.

---

## Description of any Other Options Considered and Rejected

A special education evaluation was discussed.

---

## The Reason(s) We Rejected those Options

This option was presently rejected as Laurence is making both academic and social/behavioral progress, per multiple measures including iREADY diagnostics, SAEBRS screener data, MLL services, grade report data, and teacher observations.

It was noted that, should rate of progress decrease, or an increase in problematic behaviors become present, the guidance team may be reconvened to discuss the sufficiency of a 504 plan and whether a special education evaluation may be warranted.
