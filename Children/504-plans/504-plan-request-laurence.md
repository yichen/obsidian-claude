## Requested 504 Accommodations

### 1. Academic - Reading Comprehension Support
- Text-to-speech software for reading assignments (especially informational text)
- Audiobooks for grade-level content
- Graphic organizers for making inferences and connecting text to visuals
- Pre-teaching vocabulary before new units
- Explicit instruction in informational text comprehension strategies

### 2. Academic - Working Memory Support
- **Chunked assignments** (break multi-step tasks into smaller parts with written steps)
- **Extended time** (1.5x) on all reading and writing tasks and assessments
- **Written/visual directions** for all tasks (in addition to oral)
- **Repeat and clarify instructions** as needed
- **Visual checklists and schedules** to reduce memory load
- **Copy of teacher notes** or guided notes to reduce cognitive demand

### 3. Academic - Writing Support
- Assistive technology with grammar/punctuation checking
- Sentence frames and writing templates
- Allow oral responses as alternative to written when appropriate
- Break writing assignments into smaller steps (brainstorm → draft → edit)

### 4. ADHD & Attention Support
- **Preferential seating** (front of class, away from distractions, near positive role models)
- **Movement breaks** every 20-30 minutes
- **Fidget tools** during instruction
- **Quiet, separate testing space** for assessments
- **Reduced visual clutter** in workspace

### 5. Behavioral & Emotional Regulation Support
- **Check-ins with school counselor** (regular schedule + as needed)
- **Designated cool-down space** when feeling overwhelmed
- **Advance warning** of transitions and schedule changes (2-5 minute warnings)
- **Social skills instruction** for positive peer interactions
- **Positive Behavior Intervention Plan (PBIP)** with:
  - Clear behavioral expectations
  - Strategies for de-escalation
  - Replacement behaviors for physical aggression
  - Communication plan between home and school

### 6. Anxiety Support
- Permission to take breaks when anxious or overwhelmed
- Access to calming strategies/tools (breathing exercises, sensory tools)
- Predictable routines with visual supports
- Option to respond privately rather than in front of class

### 7. Assessment Modifications
- Extended time (1.5x) on all assessments
- Separate, quiet testing space
- Breaks during long assessments
- Oral responses as alternative to written (to separate comprehension from writing ability)
- Reduce number of questions/problems per page to minimize visual overload

### 8. Specialized Intervention & Progress Monitoring
- **Daily specialized reading intervention** using evidence-based dyslexia programs (Orton-Gillingham or Wilson Reading System)
- Small group or 1:1 reading instruction focusing on informational text comprehension
- **Monthly progress monitoring** (iReady or similar) to track intervention effectiveness
- **Behavioral monitoring** with regular communication between teacher, counselor, and parents
