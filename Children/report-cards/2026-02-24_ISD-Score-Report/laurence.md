A survey completed three times per year by classroom teachers. It provides insight into social, academic, and emotional functioning. It is not a diagnostic tool.

Areas Assessed
	•	Social: Turn-taking, respect, peer relationships
	•	Academic: Attention, participation, independence, engagement
	•	Emotional-Behavioral: Mood, anxiety, willingness to try new things

Support Levels

	•	Some Need: May benefit from additional support
