- Two-word .com domains are almost impossible to find available — need a suffix (labs, tech) or three words

- "Split" in a brand name carries negative divorce connotation — avoid for coparenting products
- Names like "loom," "forge," "earnest" feel medieval/artisan — user prefers modern, clean names
- The pattern [adjective][noun]labs.com has good availability: jadeleaplabs.com, longleaplabs.com, boldshiftlabs.com all available
- Chinese-inspired naming works if the Chinese element (jade, bamboo, crane) is paired with a modern/action word (leap, path, bridge) rather than traditional (garden, palace, gate)

## 09:30 — Personal Finance App Naming and Final Domain Decisions

**What was done**:
- Registered bambooleap.com as umbrella company domain
- Ran multi-agent naming exercise for personal finance app (Branding Manager, Marketing Manager, Product Manager brainstormed 60 names)
- Domain Broker checked 80+ domains across .com, .app, .ai — single dictionary words are universally taken
- Trademark Agent reviewed top 5 candidates — killed trove.app (Trove Finance 400K users, Microsoft Class 9 trademark) and fathom.app (Fathom Analytics collision)
- Final decision: rebalancefi.com (registered on Cloudflare)

**What was learned**:
- Single dictionary word .com domains are all taken (registered in 1990s). Single word .app domains are mostly taken too. Single word .ai domains are mostly taken.
- Trademark risk is the hidden killer: "trove" has 6+ fintech conflicts. Always check trademarks BEFORE falling in love with a name.
- Compound neologisms (bambooleap, rebalancefi) are inherently safer for trademarks than dictionary words
- Multi-agent naming works well: different lenses (brand, marketing, product, legal) catch different issues. Branding loved "trove" but Trademark killed it.
- "Fi" suffix (like Wi-Fi, Lo-Fi) is clean, signals finance, and helps create unique compound names

**What was decided**:
- **bambooleap.com** = umbrella company (Bamboo Leap)
- **fairbridge.app** = coparenting app (FairBridge)
- **rebalancefi.com** = personal finance app (RebalanceFi)
- Multi-agent team debate format is effective for naming — will reuse
