## 15:XX — fairbridge ACH Case Study from OFW Transaction Data

**What was done**:
- Queried all OurFamilyWizard OFPay transactions from the finance DB
- Computed hypothetical Stripe ACH fees (0.8%, capped at $5.00) per transaction
- Ran two analyses: all available data (Jan 2025 – Jan 2026), then 12 full months (Feb 2025 – Jan 2026)
- Saved 12-month analysis to `~/personal/fairbridge/ACH-case-study-2025.md`

**What was learned**:
- OFW/OFPay transactions live in `fidelity_cma_transactions`, NOT the main `transactions` table
- CMA description for outgoing OFPay: "DEBIT OurFamilyWizar OurFamilyW"
- CMA description for incoming OFPay: "Deposit Ourfamilywiz Ourfamilyw"
- Stripe ACH fee formula: `MIN(amount * 0.008, 5.00)` — cap hits at $625+
- 52 transactions over 12 months: 25 out, 27 in; $115.50 total fees, $9.63/mo avg
- Mar 2025 was the outlier month ($24.83) due to 7 outgoing payments including large one-time items
